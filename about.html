About

This web project is a tool for documenting the 78 days of type exploration through the character ‘A’. Through the project, Exploring the Glyphs: Day In, Day Out, a new iteration of a simple character encourages creative expression through material, process, and direction. For my 78 iterations, I wanted to explore the high contrast of black and white and the usage of values. The post-production of each day encouraged a different outlook on photographing my letters, through experimenting with shadows, angles, and materials.

I wanted to create an explorative and creative vehicle for my letters, representing the same joy I had while creating my 78 iterations. The most memorable were the ones that had come by surprise or were ever-so-slightly visible, making the high-contrast editing emphasize those minimal characteristics of ‘A’. The generator gives each iteration a highlight and context into its final form.
